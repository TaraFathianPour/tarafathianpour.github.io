My Top 5 Favorite Marvel Movies

While it's nearly impossible to choose from the vast Marvel Cinematic Universe, a few films have truly earned a permanent spot in my heart. They are the ones I can rewatch anytime, offering the perfect blend of character, spectacle, and heart. If I had to narrow it down, my top five favorite Marvel movies would be:


  1. Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015)

  2. Thor: Ragnarok (2017)

  3. Black Panther (2018)

  4. Black Widow2021

  5. The Marvels2023


My Favorite Hero

Tony Stark, the genius billionaire playboy philanthropist, is my favorite Marvel hero. Unlike other heroes, he doesn't rely on super-soldier serums or god-like powers. He is a self-made hero, using his own intellect and resources to build the Iron Man armor and protect the world. His journey from a selfish weapons manufacturer to a selfless hero who is willing to make the ultimate sacrifice is what makes his character so compelling and human.
